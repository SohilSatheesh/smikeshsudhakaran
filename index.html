About Me

I am an experienced technical writer who has knack for creating various documentation and video tutorials. I am a technical writer who brings with him an experience of 8 years in software domain. I am passionate about creating user-friendly and informative technical documentation. My expertise lies in translating complex technical concepts into clear and concise language that is easily understood by end-users by working with cross-functional teams.


As part of my career, I have developed skills in Microsoft Word, Adobe FrameMaker, AuthorIT, Wiki Confluence, swagger, Visio, and draw.io. My experience includes developing user manuals, API documentation, command line interface documentation, online help systems, release notes, and training materials. I am comfortable working with subject matter experts around the globe to gather information, conduct research, and develop documentation that meets the needs of both technical and non-technical audiences.

My attention to detail, commitment to accuracy, and commitment to quality have enabled me to produce high-quality technical documentation. I take pride in my ability to work collaboratively with team members to meet project timelines and deliverables while maintaining a high level of quality. I am always seeking opportunities to learn and grow. I am excited about the possibility of joining a dynamic organization where I can contribute my skills and knowledge to create as a technical writer, innovative and effective technical documentation.
